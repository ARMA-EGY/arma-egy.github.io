About Ajdar Mob

Company Vision

Ajdar Mob is expanding its market leadership in the memory field through superior technology and significant cost savings. This creates exciting prospects. We are expanding our highly profitable premium products, mainly to mobile devices and accelerate process transformation while also focusing on developing next generation products. Our extensive system integration work, supported by increased demand for AP and CIS sensors, has shown significant growth and cost saving leadership, allowing us to launch next generation products faster.
